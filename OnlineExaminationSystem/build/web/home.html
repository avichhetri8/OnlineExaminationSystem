Why did I choose the I.J. Pioneer?


            Ashok Adhikari             Executive Board Member

I have many reasons to extend my gratification to I. J. Pioneer School family. I am a person of educational background itself. I worked more than 20 years in education sector as a founder of school. However, I enrolled my both children Solomon Adhikari and my daughter Angelina Adhikari at I. J. Pioneer School. Both of them were in this School from Nursery.

           Solomon Adhikari             Batch Year: - 2072

I am forever thankful to I.J. pioneer family. My time in I.J. Pioneer College has enriched my life in many ways. It has provided me with the necessary tools to enhance my skill, taught me more valuable knowledge about this diverse world and people. I like I.J. Pioneer for its various academic activities, extracurricular activities, intellectual awakening and freedom.
