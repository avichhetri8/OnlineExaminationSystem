Transportation


The School provides transportation facilities to the students from different location of Kathmandu valley. Parents should mention whether or not they would like to use the school transport at the time of admission. The following rules should strictly be followed by those who use school transport.Children should be escorted up to the specified bus stop and should be handed over to the bus stop. The companion should come to the bus station to pick the students after the school hours. If the school staff does not see the acquaintance, the child will be taken back to the school and the concerned parents will have to pick up the child from the school.The attendant should always carry the student’s identity card. This card is required to use the bus and receive the child after school hours.The parents should introduce the escort to the school staff in the beginning of the school session and should notify the school in case of change of escort.The transport charge depends on the child pick up distance from the school and the parents will be notified of the cost in the beginning of the session.An escort should take the child up to the school gate every day and pick him/her up from the school after the school hours. This rule will be applicable for the walking students.

Day Boaders


The facility of day boarder has been provided to the students of classes 1 to 10. I.J. has a large number of children coming from houses where both the parents are involved in services and therefore cannot spare much time to help their children right after school. For helping such parents, we have arranged this facility to the needy students. It is compulsory for the students of classes 8/9/10.Such students are abide by the following rules:The students must reach school at 7.00 am.After 2 hours of guided study, children will have lunch at 9.00 am.They will be given tea and snacks at 3.30 pm.Guided study starts from 4.00pm till 6.00pm and students can return home.No special bus facilities shall be provided for the boarders to pick up and drop.
